Informatic Tools: GCViT

GCViT is a multiple chromosome visualisation tool for visualizing resequencing or SNP array data. GCViT permits comparison of multiple accessions to a reference accession within a single dataset, to visually identify regions of similarities and differences across the genome.

View a video tutorial.

GCViT is currently available for the folowing genera:

  • Phaseolus (common bean)
  • Cicer (chickpea)
  • Arachis @ PeanutBase
  • Glycine @ SoyBase
