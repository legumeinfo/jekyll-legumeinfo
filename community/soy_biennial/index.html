Biennial Conference - Molecular & Cellular Biology of the Soybean

The Molecular & Cellular Biology of the Soybean conference is held biennially, talking place at a different venue upon each occasion.
Discussions and presentations cover topics of cutting edge research into pests and diseases, genetic and genomic translations driving soybean advancement, seed composition and nutritions, biotic interactions, functional genomics, genome structure and evolution, and more.

2025 Biennal Conference

The 19th Biennial Cellular and Molecular Biology of the Soybean Conference (Soy2025), will be held on July 23 - July 26, 2025 at University of Wisconsin - Madison campus. Registration costs, lodging and travel will be relatively low. The Soy2025 Conference precedes the ASPB annual meeting, Plant Biology 2025.
