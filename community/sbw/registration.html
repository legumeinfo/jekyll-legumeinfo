Poster Presentation and Judging Information

The poster session and graduate student poster competition will be held in conjunction with the evening social hour. Anyone is welcome to display a poster. Cash prizes will be awarded to the winners Wednesday morning of the Workshop. Please be sure to submit poster abstracts by January 10 to be considered for a 15-minute oral presentation to occur Wednesday morning. Please register to submit a poster abstract.

  • Anyone is welcome to display a poster. Posters previously presented at other meetings are welcome.
  • Poster display space is nominally 36 inches by 48 inches
  • Poster abstracts will be accepted until January 31, 2025
  • Posters may be hung up as early as Monday morning, and must remain up until Tuesday evening in order to be considered for competition awards.
  • Posters must be removed by noon on Wednesday.
  • Student Posters will be judged and awards given for the 1st ($300), 2nd ($200), 3rd ($100) place winners.
  • Awards will be presented on Wednesday morning during the opening Welcome and Announcements. If you are not present, you will be notified by email and the award will be sent to you by mail.
  • For questions about the poster session please contact Nicole Choquette

From Data to Insight: Effective Visualization Techniques in Science

This interactive workshop offers a comprehensive introduction to data visualization techniques specifically tailored for plant breeding and biological research. Open to participants of all skill levels, the workshop will delve into key principles for creating clear, effective, and reproducible visualizations that enhance scientific communication. Participants will learn how to select the most appropriate types of visualizations for various data sets, design compelling figures, and adapt visual content for different platforms, including manuscripts, conference posters, and presentations.
