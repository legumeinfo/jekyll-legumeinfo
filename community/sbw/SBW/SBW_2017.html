Agenda for USB Composition Workshop

February 15th and 16th, 2017 | St. Louis, MO | Sheraton Westport Chalet Hotel

The 2017 USB Composition Workshop highlights current research on soybean seed composition and quality traits and includes presentations on the value and delivery of soybean quality traits to the market. The workshop will begin immediately after the Soybean Breedersâ€™ Workshop and thus begin Wednesday February 15th at 11:00 am. It will be in the St Moritz/Bern conference room of the Westport Sheraton Chalet Hotel.
