Agenda for USB Seed Composition Workshop

February 24th and 25th, 2016 | St. Louis, MO | Sheraton Westport Chalet Hotel

The 2016 USB Seed Composition Workshop highlights current research on soybean seed composition and quality traits and presentations on the value and delivery of soybean quality traits to the market. The workshop will begin immediately after the Soybean Breeders' Workshop and thus begin Wednesday February 24th, 2016 at 11:00 am. It will be in the St Moritz/Bern conference room of the Sheraton Chalet Hotel.
