Datasets for Download

All of the files housed by the Legume Information System are available at the LIS Datastore.

The LIS Datastore is organized by Genus and species, with individual datasets under species directories for: genomes, annotations, expression, GWAS, QTL studies, genetic markers, synteny, diversity, and others. Each dataset contains a README file which describes that dataset.

To download multiple files or datasets, click to the left of the items you want. These will be highlighted in blue. Then click the down arrow in the upper right.
