LIS - the Legume Information System

LIS integrates genetic and genomic information across many legume species. Access this information by selecting a genus under TAXA, or a tool or method under TOOLS, or select DOWNLOAD via the Data Store. You can also explore most of the data via the rich toolsets at the various legume InterMine instances (also accessed throughout this website).

Taxa - Resources for legumes at various taxonomic levels (species, genera, and the family as a whole): data sets, genome browsers, pan-gene sets, synteny visualizations, sequence and keyword search, etc.

Tools - Pan-legume tools: sequence and keyword search, gene families and phylogenies, synteny visualization, etc. search, etc.

Download - Browse and download data sets from the LIS Data Store.

Funding and Development

LIS is funded by the USDA-ARS. LIS is developed and hosted by the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR) and the USDA-ARS SoyBase and Legume Clade Database group at Ames, IA, with the help of many other researchers.
