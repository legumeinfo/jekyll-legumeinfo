HAPMAP2 - Medicago Analysis Portal

We are developing a Medicago Hapmap as part of an international consortium consisting of the University of Minnesota, the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR), the Boyce Thompson Institute (BTI), the J. Craig Venter Institute (JCVI), Hamline University, the University of Southern California, INRA-Montpellier, ENSAT-Toulouse, and the Noble Foundation. Please select from the links below, or use the browse and search panel on the left side of the page.

Tools - Genome Browsers, Intermine and BLAST services

Download - Browse and download Medicago data sets from the LIS Data Store.

Funding and Development

HAPMAP was funded by the NSF and is powered by data from Legume Federation Data Store. The site is maintained and developed by the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR) with funding from the USDA and LIS.
