Tepary bean genomes added to LIS

Posted by Sam Hokin on 01 Oct 2021.

Phaseolus acutifolius (tepary bean) is a drought- and heat-tolerant crop native to the American Southwest and Mexico; it joins the growing set of annotated genomes at LIS, with a cultivated and wild accession described in Moghaddam et al. 2021. The cultivated accession has been added as the primary representative of the species.
